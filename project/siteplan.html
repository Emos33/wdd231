Abuja Chamber of Commerce

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Abuja Chamber of Commerce

The Abuja Chamber of Commerce website represents a local organization that supports businesses, entrepreneurs, and investors in Abuja, Nigeria. The site promotes networking opportunities, local business visibility, and economic development within the community.

Optional Domain: abujachamber.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Abuja Chamber of Commerce website is to promote local businesses and provide information about the economic activities within the city. The website will highlight member businesses, share community events, provide local information, and allow organizations to join the chamber through an online membership form.

Target Audience

The target audience includes business owners, entrepreneurs, investors, tourists, and residents who want to learn about local businesses, events, and economic opportunities in Abuja.

Scenarios

  • Which businesses in Abuja are members of the Chamber of Commerce?
  • What are the benefits of joining the Abuja Chamber of Commerce?
  • What events or networking opportunities are happening in the city?
  • How can I apply to become a member of the Chamber of Commerce?

Color Schema

  • Dark Blue (#1a4fa3) - Used for headings, navigation bar, and logo.
  • Gold (#f2b705) - Used for highlights, buttons, and accents.
  • Light Gray (#f4f6f9) - Used for page background.
  • White - Used for content sections.

Typography

  • Arial - Used for body text and paragraphs.
  • Georgia - Used for headings and section titles.
  • Verdana - Used for navigation menu and smaller text.

Website Pages

  • Home Page - Highlights community information, weather, business spotlights, and calls to action encouraging businesses to join.
  • Discover Page - Presents the history, demographics, culture, and important attractions in Abuja with images.
  • Directory Page - Displays a list of local businesses that are members of the chamber.
  • Join Page - Provides membership information and a form for businesses to apply to join the chamber.
  • References Page - Lists sources of images and information used in the website.

Membership Levels

  • Non-Profit Membership - for charities and community organizations with basic listing in the business directory.
  • Silver Membership - Includes directory listing, training opportunities, and networking events.
  • Gold Membership - Includes advertising opportunities, event sponsorships, social media promotion, and business spotlight features.

JavaScript Features

The website will include dynamic features such as:

  • Weather information displayed dynamically on the home page.
  • A business directory generated using JavaScript arrays.
  • A toggle view option (grid/list) for the directory page.
  • Form validation for the Join membership form.
  • Using localStorage to store user preferences.
